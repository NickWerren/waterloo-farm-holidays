## Camborne Produce Market

Traditional town market regarded as one of Cornwall's best, with a wide variety of local produce, household goods, and artisan products.

**When:** Every Saturday, 8:30am - 1:00pm
**Location:** Camborne town center
**Type:** Traditional market
