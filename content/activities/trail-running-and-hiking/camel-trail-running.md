The Camel Trail from Padstow to Bodmin offers up to 18 miles of flat surface, perfect for long steady runs.

**Location:** Padstow to Bodmin
**Distance:** Up to 18 miles
**Difficulty:** Easy - flat surface
**Best for:** Long steady runs, tempo runs
