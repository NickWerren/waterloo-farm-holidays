## Parkrun - Eden Project

Cornwall's most popular parkrun with an average of 224 runners each week. Free, timed 5k run/walk/jog around the spectacular Eden Project site.

**When:** Every Saturday, 9:00am
**Location:** Eden Project, Bodelva
**Type:** Community running event
**Cost:** Free (one-time registration at parkrun.org.uk required)
**Note:** All ages and abilities welcome
