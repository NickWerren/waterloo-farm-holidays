## Coastal Cliffs

Various locations throughout North Cornwall offer excellent seabird watching opportunities.

### Species to Look For

- Puffins (May-June on offshore islands)
- Gannets
- Guillemots
- Razorbills
- Kittiwakes

**Location:** Throughout North Cornwall
**Best time:** May-June for puffins
**Best for:** Seabird colonies
