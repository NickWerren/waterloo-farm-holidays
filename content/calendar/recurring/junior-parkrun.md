## Junior Parkrun

Free, fun, timed 2k runs for children aged 4-14, held at various locations across Cornwall every Sunday morning.

**When:** Every Sunday, 9:00am
**Location:** Various locations in Cornwall
**Type:** Children's running event
**Ages:** 4-14 years
**Cost:** Free (one-time registration at parkrun.org.uk required)
**Note:** Children must be accompanied by an adult
