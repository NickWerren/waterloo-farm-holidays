## Launceston Agricultural Show

A traditional Cornish agricultural show held at Kennards House, just minutes from Waterloo Farm. Featuring livestock competitions, over 200 trade stands, rural skills demonstrations, and free entertainment including live music, magicians, and face painters. A proper family day out and a real taste of Cornish country life.
