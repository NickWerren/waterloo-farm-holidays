## Francines

One of the closest fish and chip shops to Waterloo Farm, Francines in Launceston is well known for generous portions and chips fried in beef dripping. Consistently rated among the best in the area.

**Location:** Launceston (also branches in Newquay and St Austell)

**Specialties:** Traditional fish and chips fried in beef dripping, generous portions
