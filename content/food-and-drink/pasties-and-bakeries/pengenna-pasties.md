## Pengenna Pasties

Making pasties from original family recipes since 1985, with shops along the North Cornwall coast. The Tintagel shop has an on-site restaurant and tea garden. Six flavours available: traditional steak, steak and stilton, lamb, vegetable, cheese and onion, and vegan.

**Location:** Atlantic Road, Tintagel (also in Bude and St Ives)

**Specialties:** Traditional handmade Cornish pasties, cream teas at the Tintagel tea garden
