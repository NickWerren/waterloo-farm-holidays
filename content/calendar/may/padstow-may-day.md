## Padstow May Day (Obby Oss)

The famous Obby Oss festival - one of the oldest surviving May Day celebrations in Britain. The town comes alive with processions, music, and dancing as the Obby Oss parades through the streets.

**Date:** May 1, 2026
**Location:** Padstow
**Type:** Traditional Celtic celebration
**Note:** Padstow is extremely busy on this day - book accommodation well in advance
