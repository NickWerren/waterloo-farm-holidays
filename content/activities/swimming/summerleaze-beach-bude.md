## Summerleaze Beach & Sea Pool, Bude

**Distance:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Beach & Tidal Sea Pool

Unique tidal sea pool plus sandy beach - perfect for families and safe swimming.

### Overview

Summerleaze is Bude's main town beach, featuring the unique Bude Sea Pool - a tidal pool that fills with fresh seawater at high tide. This combination of sheltered pool swimming and open beach offers something for everyone, from young children to serious swimmers.

### The Sea Pool

- **Fills at high tide** - Fresh seawater twice daily
- **Protected swimming** - Sheltered from waves
- **Perfect for children** - Safe, shallow areas
- **Free to use** - Community facility
- **35m length** - Good for lane swimming
- **Beautiful setting** - Ocean views

### The Beach

- **Lifeguarded** - Summer season
- **Sandy beach** - Good for families
- **Good facilities** - Full town amenities
- **Works all tides** - Pool and beach options
- **Surf zone** - Can surf here too

### Facilities

- **Full town amenities** - Everything you need
- **Showers** - Outdoor showers available
- **Toilets** - Beach facilities
- **Cafés** - Multiple options nearby
- **Parking:** Town car parks (pay & display)
- **Surf Hire** - Available
- **Shops** - Town center close

### Swimming Options

**Sea Pool (High Tide):**
- Calm, protected water
- 1-2 hour swim window around high tide
- Suitable for all abilities
- Children can play safely

**Beach Swimming:**
- Open water swim
- Lifeguarded in summer
- Between flags only
- More challenging conditions

### Best For

- **Families with young children** - Sea pool is perfect
- **Learning to swim** in the sea safely
- **Fitness swimming** - Pool good for lengths
- **Beginners** - Protected environment
- **All weather** - Pool works in most conditions

### Safety

- **Sea Pool:**
  - Check tide times
  - 1-2 hours either side of high tide is best
  - Can have currents when filling/emptying
  - Seaweed can make rocks slippery

- **Beach:**
  - Swim between lifeguard flags
  - Understand rip currents
  - Can have surf - check conditions

### Best Time

- **High Tide** - For sea pool swimming
- **Summer** - Warmest water, lifeguards
- **Early Morning** - Quieter pool
- **Sunset** - Beautiful evening swims

### Tide Times

Essential for sea pool - check:
- BBC Weather
- Tides4Fishing.com
- Tourist information
- We can provide tide tables at the farm
