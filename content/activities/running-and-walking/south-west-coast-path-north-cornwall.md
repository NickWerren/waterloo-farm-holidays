## South West Coast Path - North Cornwall Section

The North Cornwall section of the South West Coast Path offers approximately 140 miles of dramatic coastal walking with over 2,500m of ascent.

### Key Highlights

- Dramatic cliffs and coves
- Tintagel Castle
- Port Isaac to Polzeath (10 miles)
- Padstow to Trevone
- Harlyn Bay to Padstow (7-8 hours)

### Key Sections

- Bude to Boscastle
- Boscastle to Tintagel
- Port Isaac to Padstow
- Padstow to Newquay

**Distance:** Approx 140 miles in North Cornwall
**Best for:** Experienced hikers, coastal scenery
