## Bude Farmers' Market

Scenic weekly market set alongside the beautiful Bude Canal, featuring local produce and crafts from North Cornwall producers.

**When:** Every Friday, 10:00am - 3:00pm
**Season:** Good Friday through end of September
**Location:** Bude Canal
**Type:** Farmers market
