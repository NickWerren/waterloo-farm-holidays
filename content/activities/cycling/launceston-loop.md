## Launceston Loop

**Distance:** 25 miles | **Type:** Road

A scenic circular route taking in the market town of Launceston and surrounding countryside.

### Highlights

- Historic Launceston Castle
- Rolling countryside views
- Quiet country lanes
- Traditional Cornish villages

### Route Details

**Starting Point:** Waterloo Farm
**Terrain:** Undulating with some climbs
**Best Time:** Spring to Autumn
**Estimated Time:** 2-3 hours

### Description

This circular route starts from Waterloo Farm and takes you through beautiful Cornish countryside to the historic market town of Launceston. The route features quiet country lanes with occasional climbs offering rewarding views across the rolling landscape.

### Points of Interest

- **Launceston Castle** - Norman castle with panoramic views
- **St. Mary Magdalene Church** - Ornate 16th-century church
- **Traditional villages** - North Petherwin, South Petherwin
- **Countryside views** - Rolling farmland and hedgerows

### What to Bring

- Water and snacks
- Basic repair kit
- Layered clothing
- Mobile phone
- OS Map or GPS device

**Perfect for riders with moderate fitness looking for a half-day ride exploring local countryside and history.**
