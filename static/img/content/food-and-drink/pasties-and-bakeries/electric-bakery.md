## Electric Bakery

A small artisan bakery in Bude producing sourdough bread, natural yeast pastries, and seasonal food, all made in-house. They cure their own locally sourced bacon for what are widely considered some of the best bacon sandwiches in the area. Outdoor seating, dog-friendly.

**Location:** Unit 2 The Depot, Kings Hill, Bude, EX23 8PQ

**Specialties:** Sourdough bread, natural yeast pastries, specialty coffee, bacon sandwiches. Open Wednesday to Sunday, 9am-2pm
