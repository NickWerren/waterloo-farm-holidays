## Cornwall Spring Flower Show

Known as the 'Chelsea of the South West', this spectacular two-day flower show showcases the best of Cornish horticulture at the Royal Cornwall Showground. Competitive floral exhibits, show gardens, expert talks, nursery stalls, and family activities. Dogs on leads welcome.

**Date:** March 28-29, 2026
**Location:** Royal Cornwall Showground, Whitecross, near Wadebridge
**Type:** Horticultural exhibition
