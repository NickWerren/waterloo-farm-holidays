Mounts Bay

Mounts Bay provides superb coastal birding opportunities with excellent chances to observe winter sea ducks, divers, and grebes. The bay’s sheltered waters attract a variety of seabirds throughout the year.

Target Species

  • Great Northern Diver
  • Red-throated Diver
  • Black-throated Diver
  • Eider
  • Common Scoter
  • Purple Sandpiper
  • Slavonian Grebe
  • Red-breasted Merganser
  • Various gull species

Best Viewing Locations

The bay can be viewed from multiple points along the coast road between Penzance and Marazion. Morning sessions typically offer the best viewing conditions with good light and calmer seas.

When to Visit

Winter months (November-March) are prime time for divers and sea ducks. Migration periods (spring and autumn) can bring additional species.
