## Parkrun - Heartlands

5k run through Cornwall's mining heritage site at Heartlands near Redruth, combining fitness with industrial history.

**When:** Every Saturday, 9:00am
**Location:** Heartlands, near Redruth
**Type:** Community running event
**Cost:** Free (one-time registration at parkrun.org.uk required)
