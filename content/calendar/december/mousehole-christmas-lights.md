## Mousehole Christmas Lights

A beloved Cornish Christmas tradition since 1963. Spectacular illuminated figures and displays are strung across the tiny harbour at Mousehole, visible from around 5pm to 11pm each evening from the switch-on in mid-December through to early January. One of Cornwall's most magical sights — arrive early as parking fills up fast.

**Date:** Mid-December 2026 to early January 2027 (TBC)
**Location:** Mousehole Harbour, near Penzance
**Type:** Seasonal display
