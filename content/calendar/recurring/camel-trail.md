## The Camel Trail

Cornwall's most popular traffic-free walking and cycling trail, following 18 miles of disused railway line from Padstow through Wadebridge to Bodmin.

**When:** Accessible daily, daylight hours
**Location:** Padstow to Bodmin via Wadebridge
**Distance:** 18 miles (one way)
**Type:** Multi-use trail
**Bike Hire:** Available in Padstow and Wadebridge
**Perfect For:** Families, cyclists, walkers of all abilities
