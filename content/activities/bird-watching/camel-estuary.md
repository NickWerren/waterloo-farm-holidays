## Camel Estuary

Accessible via the Camel Trail with hides available. Excellent for waders, gulls, and wildfowl in winter.

### Species to Look For

- Oystercatchers
- Curlews
- Little Egrets
- Teals and Wigeons (winter)
- Occasional ospreys (spring/autumn)

### Facilities

Hide located 1.5 miles from Wadebridge on Camel Trail
