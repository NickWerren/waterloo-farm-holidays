## Pentire Point & The Rumps

Coastal headland accessible via coastal path. Excellent for seabirds and birds of prey.

### Species to Look For

- Fulmars
- Shags
- Kestrels
- Peregrine falcons
- Passing skuas

**Location:** Near Polzeath
**Access:** Coastal path
**Best for:** Seabirds and birds of prey
