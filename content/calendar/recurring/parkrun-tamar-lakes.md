## Parkrun - Tamar Lakes

Scenic flat 5k course around Tamar Lakes near Bude, perfect for runners of all abilities with beautiful reservoir views.

**When:** Every Saturday, 9:00am
**Location:** Tamar Lakes, near Bude
**Type:** Community running event
**Cost:** Free (one-time registration at parkrun.org.uk required)
