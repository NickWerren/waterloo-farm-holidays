## Bodmin & Wenford Santa Specials

A magical festive steam train experience on the Bodmin & Wenford heritage railway. Families ride through the Cornish countryside to meet Santa — every child receives a gift. Private Santa carriage experiences with Cornish cream teas also available on select evenings. Book early as these sell out fast.
