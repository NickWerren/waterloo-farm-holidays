## Open Water Swimming Groups

Regular group sea swimming sessions at various locations around North Cornwall, welcoming swimmers of all abilities.

**When:** Multiple times weekly (tide dependent)
**Locations:** Port Gaverne, Falmouth, St Ives, and other beaches
**Type:** Group swimming
**Note:** Contact local groups for specific times and locations
**Equipment:** Wetsuit hire available locally
