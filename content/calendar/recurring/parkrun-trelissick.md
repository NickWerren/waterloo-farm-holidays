## Parkrun - Trelissick

Beautiful 5k run through the stunning National Trust gardens at Trelissick near Truro, with views over the Fal estuary.

**When:** Every Saturday, 9:00am
**Location:** Trelissick, near Truro
**Type:** Community running event
**Cost:** Free (one-time registration at parkrun.org.uk required)
**Note:** National Trust parking charges may apply
