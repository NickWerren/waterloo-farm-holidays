## Cycling in Cornwall

Cornwall offers exceptional cycling for all abilities, from leisurely coastal paths to challenging moorland climbs. Quiet lanes and stunning scenery make it a premier cycling destination.

### Off-Road & Mountain Biking

Purpose-built trails at Cardinham Woods offer graded routes from family-friendly to technical. Extensive bridleway network across moorland provides natural off-road riding.

### Family Cycling

Traffic-free routes like the Camel Trail and quiet country lanes make Cornwall ideal for family cycling. Bike hire widely available including children's bikes and tag-alongs.

### Essential Information

**Weather** - Be prepared for coastal winds and changing moorland conditions

### Best Times to Ride

**Spring/Autumn** - Quieter roads, mild weather, ideal conditions

**Summer** - Longest days but busiest roads - early starts recommended

**Winter** - Can be excellent on dry days, expect shorter daylight hours
