## Meadow Lakes

A popular commercial fishery offering multiple well-stocked lakes catering to both pleasure anglers and specimen hunters. The venue provides consistent sport year-round with excellent facilities and a friendly, welcoming atmosphere.

### Fishing Information

**Species:** Carp, tench, bream, roach, rudd, perch
**Methods:** All legal coarse fishing methods permitted
**Season:** Year-round
**Day Tickets:** Available on site - no booking required for day tickets
**Facilities:** On-site parking, tackle shop, refreshments, toilets, disabled access

### The Lakes

The fishery comprises multiple lakes, each with its own character:

**Match Lake** - Regularly stocked with carp and silver fish, ideal for pleasure sessions and match fishing. Consistent sport on pole, waggler, and feeder tactics.

**Specimen Lake** - Contains larger carp and specimens. Perfect for those targeting bigger fish in a more relaxed setting.

**General Purpose Lakes** - Well-stocked mixed fisheries suitable for all abilities, from complete beginners to experienced anglers.

### What to Expect

Meadow Lakes prides itself on providing consistent sport. Regular stockings ensure good fish populations, and the well-maintained lakes offer clear, healthy fishing water. Catch rates are generally high, making it an excellent choice for pleasure fishing or introducing newcomers to the sport.

### Fishing Techniques

**Pole Fishing** - Highly effective on all lakes, particularly for pleasure anglers targeting mixed bags.

**Feeder Fishing** - Method feeders and pellet feeders work well for carp. Traditional feeders produce good catches of bream and tench.

**Float Fishing** - Waggler tactics can be very effective, particularly in warmer months.

### Facilities & Amenities

The fishery offers excellent facilities including ample parking, clean toilets, and a well-stocked tackle shop for any forgotten items or bait requirements. Hot and cold refreshments are available, making it easy to spend a full day at the venue.

### Ideal For

- Beginners and families - friendly, accessible venue
- Pleasure anglers seeking consistent sport
- Match anglers - regular competitions held
- Specimen hunters on the dedicated carp lake
- Corporate days and group bookings

### Top Tips

- Pellets and corn work consistently well across all lakes
- Early morning and evening sessions often most productive
- The tackle shop staff can advise on current hot spots and methods
- Consider booking for weekend sessions during peak season
- Perfect venue for introducing children to fishing
