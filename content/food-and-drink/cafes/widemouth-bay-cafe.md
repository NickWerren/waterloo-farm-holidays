## Widemouth Bay Cafe

A popular beachside cafe overlooking Widemouth Bay with indoor and outdoor seating. The perfect spot for a coffee and cake after a surf or beach walk. Dog-friendly with a varied menu including vegan and gluten-free options.

**Location:** Marine Drive, Widemouth Bay, Bude, EX23 0AW

**Specialties:** Breakfasts, coffee, homemade cakes, beach views. Dog-friendly
