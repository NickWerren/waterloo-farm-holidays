## Porthleven Food Festival

One of Cornwall's most popular food festivals, set around the stunning harbour at Porthleven. Three days of live cookery demonstrations, street food from top local producers, artisan stalls, and live music. A must for any foodie visiting Cornwall in spring.

**Date:** April 24-26, 2026
**Location:** Porthleven Harbour, South Cornwall
**Type:** Food and drink festival
