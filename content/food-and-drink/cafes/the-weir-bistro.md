## The Weir Bistro

Relaxed bistro right beside the weir on the Bude Canal, serving brunch, lunch, and homemade cakes. A lovely spot to stop after a walk along the canal towpath.

**Location:** Bude, beside the canal

**Specialties:** Brunch, light lunches, homemade cakes, riverside terrace
