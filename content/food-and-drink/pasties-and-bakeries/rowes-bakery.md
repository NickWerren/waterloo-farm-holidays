## Rowe's Bakery

Cornwall's largest family bakery, baking award-winning pasties since 1949. You'll find their shops across the county, with branches in Launceston and Bodmin being the closest to Waterloo Farm.

**Location:** Multiple locations including Launceston, Bodmin, Bude

**Specialties:** Award-winning traditional Cornish pasties, sausage rolls, fresh bread
