## St Gothian Sands

A productive site on Cornwall's north coast offering excellent opportunities for waders, gulls, and coastal birds. Part of popular birding routes combining with nearby Hayle Estuary.

### Target Species

- Short-eared Owl (winter)
- Lapwing
- Golden Plover
- Iceland Gull (winter)
- Glaucous Gull (winter)
- Purple Sandpiper
- Sanderling
- Various wader species

### Habitat

Sandy beach backed by dunes and grassland. The varied habitat attracts both coastal and terrestrial species, with the grassland areas particularly good for Short-eared Owls during winter.

### Access

Easy access with minimal walking required. Suitable for all abilities. Best combined with a visit to nearby Hayle Estuary for a full morning's birding.
