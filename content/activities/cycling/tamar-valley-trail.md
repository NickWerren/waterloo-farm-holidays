## Tamar Valley Discovery Trail

**Distance:** 30 miles | **Type:** Mixed

Follow the beautiful Tamar Valley through historic mining landscapes and riverside trails.

### Highlights

- River Tamar views
- Mining heritage sites
- Woodland trails
- Gentle gradients
- Wildlife spotting

### Route Details

**Starting Point:** Launceston
**Terrain:** Mostly flat with gentle hills
**Best Time:** Year-round
**Estimated Time:** 3-4 hours
**Surface:** Mixed road and trail

### Description

This scenic route follows the Tamar Valley, combining quiet lanes with sections of off-road trail. The Tamar Valley is an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty with rich mining heritage. The route is suitable for hybrid or mountain bikes due to mixed terrain.

### Points of Interest

- **River Tamar** - Natural border between Cornwall and Devon
- **Mining Heritage** - Historic copper and silver mines
- **Kit Hill** - Dramatic viewpoint (optional detour)
- **Woodland Sections** - Beautiful tree-lined trails
- **Wildlife** - Otters, kingfishers, buzzards

### Route Tips

- **Bike Type:** Hybrid or mountain bike recommended
- **Traffic:** Mostly quiet with some busier sections
- **Services:** Villages along route for refreshments
- **Terrain:** Mix of tarmac and gravel/trail sections
- **Family-Friendly:** Suitable for older children with cycling experience

### What to Bring

- Water and snacks
- Basic repair kit
- Appropriate bike for mixed terrain
- Camera for scenic spots
- Binoculars for wildlife watching
