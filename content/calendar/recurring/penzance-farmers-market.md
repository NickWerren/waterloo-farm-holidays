## Penzance Farmers' Market

Year-round weekly farmers market featuring West Cornwall produce, fresh fish, artisan breads, local vegetables, and craft stalls.

**When:** Every Friday, 9:00am - 2:00pm
**Location:** St Johns Hall, Penzance
**Type:** Farmers market
