## Welcome to Saffron Cottage

Tucked away in the corner of the courtyard is Saffron Cottage, a country style holiday cottage but with an up to date feel. All on one level - except for two steps in the corridor to the bedrooms - this cottage has our signature slate floors and beams, plus the added luxury of underfloor heating.

Enjoy bright and spacious open plan living, with a painted country kitchen, and stylish dining and relaxing areas. There is a luxurious family bathroom.

### Bedrooms

- **Master bedroom:** King sized bed
- **Second bedroom:** Twin beds

### Outside

French doors open out onto a patio which is ideal for enjoying leisurely meals outside, and a small sloping garden attracts bees and butterflies and also sometimes, our friendly trio of hens looking for crumbs!

### Amenities

- Bright open plan living
- Painted country kitchen
- Underfloor heating
- Slate floors and beams
- French doors to patio
- Luxurious family bathroom
- WiFi
- Parking

### Location

Set in the heart of the Cornish countryside at Waterloo Farm, North Petherwin, Launceston.
