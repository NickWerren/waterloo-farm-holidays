## Wadebridge Discovery Market

Weekly farmers and craft market held every Wednesday in Wadebridge town center, featuring local produce, artisan foods, crafts, and fresh flowers.

**Date:** Every Wednesday, year-round
**Location:** Wadebridge Town Center
**Type:** Farmers and craft market
**Time:** Morning to early afternoon
