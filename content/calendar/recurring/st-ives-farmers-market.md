## St Ives Farmers' Market

Award-winning farmers market featuring local produce, artisan bread, Cornish cheeses, and craft stalls in the heart of St Ives.

**When:** Every Monday, 9:30am - 2:00pm
**Location:** The Island Centre, St Ives
**Type:** Farmers market
