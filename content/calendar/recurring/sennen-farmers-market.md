## Sennen Farmers' Market

Popular weekly market with 30-50 stalls selling local produce, crafts, and artisan goods from the Land's End peninsula.

**When:** Every Tuesday, 9:30am - 12:30pm
**Location:** Sennen Community Centre
**Type:** Farmers market
