## Liberty Coffee

A Launceston specialty coffee shop just 6 miles from Waterloo Farm. Everything is made from scratch — homity pie, bakewell, courgette and lime cake — with plenty of gluten-free options. House coffee roasted by Kiss The Hippo, plus a changing lineup of guest coffees. Dog-friendly.

**Location:** North Gate Street, Launceston

**Specialties:** Specialty coffee, homemade cakes and savouries, gluten-free options
