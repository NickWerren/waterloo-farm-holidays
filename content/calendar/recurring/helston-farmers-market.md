## Helston Farmers' Market

One of Cornwall's largest and most popular farmers markets, featuring dozens of local producers, artisan foods, fresh produce, and crafts.

**When:** First Saturday of every month, 9:30am - 12:30pm
**Location:** Old Cattle Market, Helston
**Type:** Farmers market
