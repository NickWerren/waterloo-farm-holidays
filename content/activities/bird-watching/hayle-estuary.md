## Hayle Estuary

The UK's most southerly estuary and one of the few that never freezes, Hayle Estuary is managed by the RSPB and provides crucial habitat for wintering waders, waterfowl, and migrating birds.

### Target Species

- Spoonbill
- Kingfisher
- Purple Sandpiper
- Iceland Gull
- Caspian Gull
- Great Northern Diver
- Red-throated Diver
- Black-throated Diver
- Eider
- Common Scoter
- Various waders and wildfowl

### Best Time to Visit

Winter months offer the best opportunities for observing divers, gulls, and winter waders. Morning sessions typically provide optimal viewing conditions.

### Accessibility

Minimal walking required, making this location ideal for all abilities.
