## Wadebridge Discovery Market

Vibrant weekly market featuring 44 traders selling local produce, artisan foods, crafts, vintage vinyl, fresh flowers, and handmade goods. Enjoy live music while you browse.

**When:** Every Wednesday, 9:00am - 4:00pm
**Location:** Molesworth Street, Wadebridge
**Type:** Farmers and craft market
**Highlight:** Live music throughout the day
