## Local Food & Drink

Cornwall is renowned for its exceptional food scene - from fresh seafood and traditional Cornish pasties to artisan producers and award-winning restaurants. Whether you're after a hearty pub lunch, fine dining, a quick coffee, or authentic fish and chips, you'll find excellent options within easy reach of Waterloo Farm.

We've organized our recommendations by type to help you find exactly what you're looking for, from traditional Cornish pubs serving locally-sourced food to beachside cafés and family-run bakeries.

### What to Expect

**Fresh, Local Produce** - Cornwall's restaurants and pubs pride themselves on using locally-sourced ingredients, from fresh fish landed at nearby ports to meat from Cornish farms.

**Traditional Cornish Fare** - Don't leave without trying a proper Cornish pasty, fresh crab sandwiches, and locally-caught fish and chips.

**Welcoming Atmosphere** - Cornwall's food establishments are known for their friendly, welcoming service and relaxed atmosphere.

**Something for Everyone** - From fine dining to food trucks, quick snacks to leisurely meals, there's excellent food to suit all tastes and budgets.
