## The Camel Trail

The Camel Trail follows an old railway line from Padstow to Wenford Bridge via Wadebridge and Bodmin. This flat, traffic-free route is suitable for all abilities, including wheelchairs and pushchairs.

### Route Sections

- **Padstow to Wadebridge:** 5.5 miles
- **Wadebridge to Bodmin:** 6 miles
- **Bodmin to Wenford Bridge:** 6.8 miles

### Bike Hire

- Bridge Bike Hire - Eddystone Road, Wadebridge
- Multiple shops in Padstow, Wadebridge, and Bodmin

### Facilities

Bird hide halfway between Wadebridge and Padstow

### Parking

- Piggy Lane (behind Lidl Wadebridge)
- Padstow town car parks

**Distance:** 18.3 miles total
**Best for:** All abilities, wheelchairs, pushchairs
