## Explore the Great Outdoors

Cornwall and Devon offer some of the UK's most spectacular landscapes and outdoor activities. From rugged coastal paths to tranquil moorland trails, cycling quiet lanes to surfing Atlantic waves, there's something for every adventurer within easy reach of Waterloo Farm.

### What to Expect

**Routes for All Abilities** - From family-friendly coastal trails to challenging moorland climbs, activities are available for all fitness levels and experience.

**Stunning Natural Beauty** - Dramatic coastlines, peaceful moorland, ancient woodlands, and pristine beaches provide the perfect backdrop for outdoor adventures.

**Year-Round Activities** - While summer offers the longest days, spring and autumn provide quieter trails and mild conditions ideal for exploring.
