## Welcome to Sandmartins

Another large cottage with an airy and light open plan living area. French doors open out onto a private, sunny patio which is perfect for barbecuing and al fresco eating.

The kitchen has everything you will need for your holiday catering and includes a gas Rayburn and hob plus microwave plus all our standard fixtures and fittings.

### Bedrooms

There are 4 bedrooms in Sandmartins:

- **Upstairs:** Two pretty double rooms, both with en suite shower rooms, plus a bunks room with another bathroom close by
- **Downstairs:** A comfortable double bedroom with en suite wet room, also suitable for those with mobility problems

### Large Groups

This cottage can be opened up on the ground floor to link through to Haypole Barn next door, creating a 16 person holiday barn - perfect for retreats, activity courses, and family gatherings.
