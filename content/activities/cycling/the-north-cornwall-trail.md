## The North Cornwall Trail

A 40.5 mile route from Bodmin to the Devon border, mostly on road. Suitable for cyclists, walkers and horse riders.

### Key Stops

- St Breward
- Camelford
- Bude

**Distance:** 40.5 miles
**Difficulty:** Moderate - mostly on road
**Best for:** Experienced cyclists, walkers, horse riders
