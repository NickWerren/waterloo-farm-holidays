## The Rustic Tea Garden

A tucked-away tea garden near the cliffs at Northcott Mouth, with benches beside a stream and lovely coastal views. A favourite stop for walkers on the South West Coast Path between Bude and Duckpool. Dog-friendly with outdoor seating. Now also known as Sip and Sea, open March to November.

**Location:** Northcott Mouth, near Bude

**Specialties:** Cream teas, homemade cakes, hot and cold drinks. Seasonal, March to November
