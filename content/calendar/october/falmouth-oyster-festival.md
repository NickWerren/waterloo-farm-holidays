## Falmouth Oyster Festival

Four-day celebration of Cornish seafood, particularly the famous Fal oysters. Includes cooking demonstrations, tastings, live music, and the opening of the traditional oyster dredging season.

**Date:** October 8-11, 2026
**Location:** Falmouth
**Type:** Food and drink festival
**Highlight:** Traditional oyster dredging ceremony
